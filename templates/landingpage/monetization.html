Platform Monetization Guide

TikTok

Kenya is not officially included in TikTok's Creator Fund program due to local advertising market conditions. However, multiple monetization opportunities exist through alternative channels.

Available Monetization Methods:
  • Sponsored content and brand partnerships with local or international brands
  • Affiliate marketing programs with commission-based earnings
  • Virtual gifts and tips during live streaming sessions (minimum requirements apply)
  • Direct merchandise sales and product promotions
  • Content licensing and brand collaboration deals
Creator Rewards Program Requirements:
  • Associated Facebook Page with business verification
  • Minimum age requirement of 18 years
  • Valid payment method configuration (credit/debit card or PayPal)
  • Minimum threshold of 10,000 followers
  • 30,000 one-minute views on 3+ minute videos within 60 days
  • Original content compliance with Monetization Eligibility Standards
  • Facebook Page establishment for minimum 90 days
  • Consistent presence and engagement for 30+ days
Beta Program

Meta's Content Monetization Beta consolidates earning options including:

  • Reels performance bonuses
  • Enhanced in-stream ads
  • Performance-based rewards

Facebook (Meta)

Monetization Methods:
  • In-Stream Ads on Facebook - Earn from ads in original videos
  • Facebook Ads on Reels - Revenue sharing from Reels advertisements
  • Facebook Stars - Virtual gifts during live streams
Requirements:
  • Have a Facebook Page associated with your account
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Valid payment method (credit/debit card or PayPal)
  • Have at least 10,000 followers
  • Generate at least 30,000 one-minute views on 3+ minute videos in past 60 days
  • Post original content adhering to Monetization Eligibility Standards
  • Facebook Page must be at least 90 days old
  • Established presence for at least 30 days

Instagram

Comprehensive Monetization Suite:
  • Instagram Reels Play Bonus - Performance and engagement-based payments
  • Sponsored Content - Professional brand partnerships and collaborations
  • Virtual Gifts - Live stream monetization through audience engagement
  • Creator Fund Programs - Direct compensation for quality content creation
  • Shopping Features - Product tagging and affiliate commission structures
  • IGTV monetization and long-form content revenue
Platform Requirements:
  • Minimum age verification of 18 years
  • Creator or Business professional account setup
  • 10,000+ followers for subscription and Reels monetization
  • Geographic eligibility in supported monetization regions
  • Valid banking or PayPal account for payment processing
  • Strict adherence to Community Guidelines and Monetization Policies
Monetization Flexibility:

Instagram offers monetization opportunities for creators of various sizes:

  • Sponsored posts - Brand collaboration content
  • Affiliate marketing - Commission-based promotions
  • Subscriptions - Exclusive content access
  • Merchandise sales - Integrated shopping features
  • Live gifts - Real-time audience engagement
  • Crowdfunding - Community-supported projects

WhatsApp Business

Business Monetization Opportunities:
  • WhatsApp Business API - Enterprise messaging services with premium features
  • Digital Product Sales - E-books, guides, templates, and educational tutorials
  • Consultation Services - Professional one-on-one coaching and advisory sessions
  • Premium Communities - Subscription-based WhatsApp groups with exclusive content
  • Affiliate Marketing - Strategic product recommendations through status updates
  • Customer Service Solutions - Professional communication and support tools
Platform Requirements:
  • Verified WhatsApp Business account setup
  • Complete business information and verification
  • Established customer base with active WhatsApp engagement
  • Professional business profile with contact details
  • Compliance with WhatsApp Business policies
Community Building

WhatsApp offers unique opportunities for:

  • Direct customer communication
  • Premium group memberships
  • Personalized service delivery
  • Local market penetration

Threads

Emerging Monetization Features:
  • Threads Bonus Program - Performance-based compensation for viral and engaging posts
  • Brand Partnership Hub - Sponsored content and professional influencer collaborations
  • Content Creation Services - Professional writing and content strategy for businesses
  • Exclusive Communities - Premium access to specialized discussion groups and networking
  • Text-based advertising integration - Native promotional content opportunities
Platform Status: Rapidly Evolving

As Meta's newest social platform, Threads is continuously expanding its monetization capabilities:

  • Integration with existing Meta advertising ecosystem
  • Text-focused content monetization strategies
  • Professional networking and B2B opportunities
  • Cross-platform promotion with Instagram and Facebook

Content and Monetization Guidelines

Meta Platform Compliance Standards

Content Requirements & Standards:
  • Original content creation with verified intellectual property rights
  • Strict prohibition of hateful, violent, or sexually explicit material
  • Comprehensive compliance with Meta's Partner Monetization Policies
  • Adherence to Community Standards and Content Monetization guidelines
  • Avoidance of engagement manipulation and misinformation tactics
  • Advertiser-friendly content that meets brand safety requirements
Violation Consequences

Policy violations can result in:

  • Temporary monetization suspension
  • Permanent loss of monetization features
  • Account restrictions or penalties
  • Revenue payment delays or forfeitures
Best Practice:

Maintain consistent compliance to preserve monetization eligibility and build long-term revenue potential.
