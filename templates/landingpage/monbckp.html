Platform Monetization

TikTok

Kenya is not officially included in TikTok's Creator Fund program because of local advertising market conditions.

Monetization Methods:
  • Sponsored content and brand deals with local or international brands
  • Affiliate marketing by promoting products and earning commissions
  • Virtual gifts and tips during live streams (must be 18+ and have at least 1,000 followers)
  • Selling merchandise or products
Creator Rewards Program Requirements:
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a minimum of 10,000 followers
  • Have at least 100,000 video views in the last 30 days
  • Complete profile with profile picture, bio, and contact info
  • Post high-quality, original videos (at least one minute long)
  • Have an account in good standing with no violations

Payment Options: PayPal or mobile money like M-Pesa

Facebook (Meta)

Monetization Methods:
  • In-Stream Ads on Facebook - Earn from ads in original videos
  • Facebook Ads on Reels - Revenue sharing from Reels advertisements
  • Facebook Stars - Virtual gifts during live streams
Requirements:
  • Have a Facebook Page associated with your account
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Valid payment method (credit/debit card or PayPal)
  • Have at least 10,000 followers
  • Generate at least 30,000 one-minute views on 3+ minute videos in past 60 days
  • Post original content adhering to Monetization Eligibility Standards
  • Facebook Page must be at least 90 days old
  • Established presence for at least 30 days

Instagram

Monetization Methods:
  • Instagram Reels Play Bonus - Payment based on performance and engagement
  • Sponsored Content - Brand partnerships and collaborations
  • Virtual Gifts - Live stream monetization through viewer gifts
  • Creator Fund Programs - Direct payments for quality content
  • Shopping Features - Product tagging and affiliate commissions
Requirements:
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Use a Creator or Business professional account
  • Have at least 10,000 followers for subscriptions and Reels monetization
  • Live in a region where Instagram monetization features are available
  • Have a bank or PayPal account for payment processing
  • Follow Instagram's Community Guidelines and Monetization Policies

WhatsApp Business

Monetization Methods:
  • WhatsApp Business API - Businesses pay for messaging services
  • Digital Product Sales - E-books, guides, templates, tutorials
  • Consultation Services - One-on-one coaching and advice sessions
  • Paid Communities - Premium WhatsApp groups with subscription fees
  • Affiliate Marketing - Product recommendations through status updates
  • Customer Service Solutions - Professional communication tools
Requirements:
  • WhatsApp Business account setup
  • Verified business information
  • Customer base willing to engage via WhatsApp

Threads

Monetization Methods (New):
  • Threads Bonus Program - Performance-based payments for popular posts
  • Brand Partnerships - Sponsored content and influencer collaborations
  • Content Creation Services - Writing and content strategy for businesses
  • Community Building - Paid access to exclusive discussion groups

Content and Monetization Guidelines

Meta Platform Guidelines

Content Requirements:
  • Content must be original and not violate copyright or intellectual property rights
  • Content should not include hateful, violent, sexually explicit, misleading, or fraudulent material
  • Compliance with Meta's Partner Monetization Policies, Community Standards, and Content Monetization Policies is mandatory
  • Avoid engagement baiting, misinformation, and content that doesn't comply with advertiser-friendly guidelines
  • Pages and accounts with repeated violations can lose access to monetization features
