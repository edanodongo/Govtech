Collaboration & Innovation

Kenya has over the years been a host to various African and Global tech and innovation summits and conferences. These have expanded the Kenyan market and provided innovators a platform to showcase their products. As a result, a number of Kenyan start-ups have received funding from foreign investors as well as opportunities to showcase solutions on the Global market. Examples of such events include; Africa BFSI Innovation Summit, Afro Asia FinTech festival, Afro tech summit, International Development Innovation Alliance (IDIA), The Sustainable Blue Economy Conference
