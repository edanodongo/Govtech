Supporting Government Portals

Direct access to relevant government services and regulatory bodies:

  • eCitizen Portal – Central access to government services and business registration.
  • Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) – Tax registration, filing, and compliance.
  • ICT Authority – ICT sector standards, licensing, and support.
  • Communications Authority of Kenya – Communications licensing and regulation.
  • Office of the Data Protection Commissioner – Data protection compliance and guidelines

Links to Supporting Government Portals is a dedicated resource hub that connects software developers and tech startups in Kenya to essential government platforms and regulatory bodies. This section provides direct access to key services such as business registration, tax compliance, data protection, intellectual property, ICT innovation support, and fintech regulation. By centralizing these links with clear descriptions and guidance, the platform helps users navigate legal and administrative processes more efficiently, ensuring they remain compliant, protected, and well-positioned to grow within Kenya’s evolving digital economy.

Other Useful Platforms

  • KEBS: Standards for software, cybersecurity, and electronics
  • Huduma Kenya: In-person government services and documentation
